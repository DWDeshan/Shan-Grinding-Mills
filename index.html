Our Story in Matara

Shan Grinding Mills has been a cornerstone of the Matara community since 2016, providing premium quality spice grinding services with traditional methods passed down through generations.

Located at No304, Bandaranayakapura, Kekanadura, we take pride in sourcing the finest local ingredients and transforming them into aromatic spices that enhance every dish.
